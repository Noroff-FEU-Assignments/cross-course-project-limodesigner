Web Security

How would you advise a potential client to secure his/her website from web security attacks? Note: the word count for your answer should not be greater than 250 words

I would advise a client, to secure their website in the best possible way. Malicious attacks can ruin a business in minutes.
It is important to have focus on the use of strong passwords and consider implementing a two-factor authentication for interactions. Also, it is important to protect your devices, and only store the data you need from your customers. If you regulate your requests, and are selective on which requests to accept, the risks minimize. The importance of sanitize and validate input, for example by type, timeouts, or format, will be crucial. By using trusted third-party applications and keeping your website up to date, will also help you to secure your website from malicious attacks. This means that you utilise https, regularly review your code, review all plugins and third-party integrations, do backups, and have a security plan, is a good start.
